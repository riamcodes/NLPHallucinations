- Simple Factual: 12 questions

## Hallucination Detection Methodology
This dataset is designed to test generative AI models' tendency to hallucinate by:
- Providing questions across diverse categories
- Including both factual and impossible/nonsensical questions
- Marking potential hallucination risks
